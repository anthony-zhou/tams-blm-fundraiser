NO MORE.

We all have a responsibility to pour into and uplift people in the black community, especially during these painful times.

With that being said, the Black Student Union and 18 other clubs at the Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science have decided to host a raffle to raise money for various organizations that focus on investing in African-Americans and their communities. View clubs, organizations, and prizes here.
